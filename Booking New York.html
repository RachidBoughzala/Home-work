New York 29-30 April the program:

Day 1

Morning:

Start your day with a virtual tour of the Metropolitan Museum of Art, one of the world's largest and most comprehensive art museums. You can explore the museum's collections online, including works of art from ancient times to the present day, and learn about the history and significance of each piece.

Afternoon:

In the afternoon, visit the 9/11 Memorial & Museum, a powerful tribute to the victims of the September 11, 2001 terrorist attacks. You can take a virtual tour of the museum, learn about the events of that day, and hear the stories of the survivors and first responders.

Evening:

Finish your day with a virtual tour of Times Square, one of the most iconic and vibrant neighborhoods in New York City. You can explore the bustling streets, admire the colorful billboards and signs, and learn about the history of this famous intersection.


Day 2

Morning:

Start your day with a virtual tour of the American Museum of Natural History, a world-renowned institution dedicated to the study of natural history and culture. You can explore the museum's collections online, including exhibits on dinosaurs, human origins, and biodiversity.

After Notre-Dame, visit the Sainte-Chapelle, a stunning chapel located on the Île de la Cité. You can admire the beautiful stained glass windows, which are considered some of the finest in the world.

Afternoon:

In the afternoon, visit the Statue of Liberty, one of the most recognizable symbols of freedom and democracy in the world. You can take a virtual tour of the statue and its surroundings, learn about its history and significance, and enjoy the panoramic views of the city skyline.

Evening:

Finish your day with a virtual tour of the Brooklyn Bridge, one of the most iconic landmarks in New York City. You can learn about the history and engineering of the bridge, explore its pedestrian walkway, and enjoy the breathtaking views of the city from across the East River.
