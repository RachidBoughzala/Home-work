Paris 15-16 April the program:

Day 1

Morning:

Start your day with a virtual visit to the Louvre Museum, one of the world's largest and most famous museums. You can explore the Louvre's collections online, including some of the most famous works of art, such as the Mona Lisa, the Winged Victory of Samothrace, and the Venus de Milo.

After the Louvre, take a stroll through the Tuileries Garden, a beautiful park located between the Louvre and the Place de la Concorde. You can virtually walk through the gardens, admire the sculptures, and enjoy the views of the city.

Afternoon:

In the afternoon, visit the Eiffel Tower, one of the most iconic landmarks in Paris. You can take a virtual tour of the tower, learn about its history and construction, and enjoy the stunning views of the city from the top.

Evening:

Finish your day with a virtual tour of Montmartre, a picturesque neighborhood located on a hill overlooking Paris. You can explore the narrow streets, admire the colorful buildings, and learn about the area's history and famous residents, such as Vincent van Gogh and Pablo Picasso.


Day 2

Morning:

Start your day with a virtual tour of Notre-Dame Cathedral, one of the most famous Gothic cathedrals in the world. You can explore the cathedral's interior and exterior, learn about its history and architecture, and discover the stories behind its famous gargoyles.

After Notre-Dame, visit the Sainte-Chapelle, a stunning chapel located on the Île de la Cité. You can admire the beautiful stained glass windows, which are considered some of the finest in the world.

Afternoon:

In the afternoon, visit the Musée d'Orsay, a museum located in a former train station that houses a collection of Impressionist and Post-Impressionist art. You can explore the museum's collections online, including works by Monet, Renoir, Van Gogh, and Gauguin.

Evening:

Finish your day with a virtual tour of the Palace of Versailles, a magnificent royal residence located just outside of Paris. You can explore the palace's opulent rooms, learn about its history and architecture, and stroll through the beautiful gardens.
